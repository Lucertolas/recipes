Ingredients:
- 15 tablespoons of good quality flour (wholemeal, type 2, mix...);
- mother yeast;
- water as needed;
- salt;
- extra virgin olive oil as your taste prefers.

Procedure:
Mix accurately all the ingredients. Take out two or three tablespoons of dough
in order to keep the mother yeast for the next bread. Let it grow for two to
four hours. Cook in the oven at 180 Celsius degrees as much as you wish.
